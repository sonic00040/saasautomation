## Key Components:

### Contact Form Section
- **Left Column**: Detailed contact form
  - Required fields marked with *
  - Various input types (text, email, textarea, dropdown)
  - Checkbox options for additional services
  - Submit button with response time expectations

- **Right Column**: Contact information
  - Email, phone, address
  - Business hours
  - Live chat availability
  - Quick action buttons

### Quick Contact Options
- **Three specialized contact types**:
  - Sales inquiries with demo booking
  - Technical support with 24/7 availability
  - Partnership opportunities
- **Clear CTAs** for each contact type
- **Response time expectations**

### FAQ Section
- **Contact-specific questions**
- **Response times** and availability
- **Support channel guidance**
- **Career information**

### Office Locations
- **Headquarters information**
- **Interactive map placeholder**
- **Global presence indicators**
- **Multi-language support mention**

### Social Media & Newsletter
- **Social media links** for community building
- **Newsletter signup** for lead nurturing
- **Value proposition** for subscription
